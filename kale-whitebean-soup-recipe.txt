Ingredients:
2 cans white beans, drainned
10 cups chicken broth
2 celery ribs, cfinely chopped
1 onion, minced
2 carrots, peeled and sliced into half inch pieces
4 garlic cloves, pressed
1  pound kale
2 slices bacon
olive oil
salt/pepper
crushed red pepper
1 sprig rosemary

In large dutch oven:
Sautee bacon, celery, and onion in 1 table spoon olive oil over medium high heat until bacon is cripsy and veggies are softened.
Add 1 tablespoon crushed red pepper and pressed garlic and stir until fragrant.
Add broth, carrot, kale, and beans. 
Bring just to boil then reduce heat and simmmer for 15 minutes.
Sesaon with salt and pepper.
Serve with crusty bread and grated parmesean cheese.
Enjoy :)
